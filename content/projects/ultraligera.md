## ultraligera

Unofficial fan site for the Spanish rock band Ultraligera. Aggregates tour dates, discography, and links to official channels (Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube) in one place. Built entirely inside a Docker Sandbox, done in a couple of days. Non-affiliated, non-commercial, just a place to find the next show.

- Live app: [ultraligera.vercel.app](https://ultraligera.vercel.app)
