## ultraligera

Unofficial fan site for the Spanish rock band Ultraligera. The [official site](https://www.ultraligera.com/) exists but falls short, so I built the one I wish I'd found: tour dates, discography, and links to official channels (Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube) in one place. Scheduled jobs keep the data fresh on their own: YouTube view counts, Apple Music discography, and tour dates scraped from the official site. Built entirely inside a Docker Sandbox, done in a couple of days. Non-affiliated, non-commercial, just a place to find the next show.

- Live app: [ultraligera.vercel.app](https://ultraligera.vercel.app)
- Blog post: [De tablas anidadas a prompts: 25 años haciendo webs](/posts/2026-05-21-de-tablas-anidadas-a-prompts-25-anos-haciendo-webs/)
