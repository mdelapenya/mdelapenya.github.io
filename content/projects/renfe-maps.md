## renfe-maps

Real-time train tracker for Spanish railways (Renfe). Shows live train positions on Google Maps using GTFS Real-Time data, with delay indicators, station markers, service filtering, and SSE streaming. Built entirely inside a Docker Sandbox, done in a couple of days. I'm not sure how much the public administration paid to build the [largo recorrido app](https://tiempo-real.largorecorrido.renfe.com), probably a million of euros.

- Live app: [renfe-maps.vercel.app](https://renfe-maps.vercel.app)
- Blog post: [Building a Real App Inside a Docker Sandbox](/posts/2026-04-14-building-a-real-app-inside-a-docker-sandbox/)
